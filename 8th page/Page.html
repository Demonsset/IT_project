Ancient ROME

A powerful civilization that grew on the Italian Peninsula and became one of the greatest empires in history. It began as a small city and then expanded into a vast empier that controlled large parts of Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East.

  • Traditionally in 753 BC
  • Famous Leaders: Julius Caesar, Augustus, Constantine
  • Government: Kingdom, Republic, then Empier
  • Language: Latin
  • Army: Highly trained Roman Legions
  • Famous Landmarks: Colosseum, Pantheon, Roman Forum
  • Architecture: Arches, domes, public baths
  • Culture: Theater, gladiator games, festivals
  • Economy: Trade across Europe, Africa, and Asia
  • Religion: Began with Roman gods, later Christinaty spread
