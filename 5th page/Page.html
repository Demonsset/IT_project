Spain: A Journey Through Time

Spain's history and monuments tell the story of a great civilization. From the golden age of Al-Andalus to the towering cathedrals of the medieval era, Spain left the world treasures of engineering, art, and philosophy. Here you can discover the secrets of these timeless landmarks that still astonish the world.

The Alhambra is a vast Moorish palace and fortress complex sitting on a hilltop above Granada. Built in the 13th century, it is considered one of the greatest achievements of Islamic architecture in the world.

The Sagrada Família is Gaudí's unfinished masterpiece — a cathedral under construction since 1882. Its soaring spires and organic stone carvings make it the most visited monument in Spain.

Sagrada Família
